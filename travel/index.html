Italy- Travellors choice

Whatever you want to experience on your trip, trains in Italy make itinerary planning easy. Thanks to Trenitalia's and Italo's excellent high-speed train services, it's also super fast to get around. It never takes more than an hour or two to travel between Italy's major cities. And going by train means you arrive right in the city centre, so you can start exploring as soon as you arrive!
